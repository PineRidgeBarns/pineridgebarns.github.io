Delivery Information

Sheds

Our sheds are built by the Amish in Lancaster County, PA. They are trucked to our area via tractor-trailers, and then delivered locally by our delivery vehicles. We service an 80-mile radius from our business in Millsboro, DE 19966. Due to the size, weight, and bulk of these sheds it makes in financially infeasible to ship these products out of this area.
